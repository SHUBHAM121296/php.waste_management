About Us

The long-recognized hierarchy of management of wastes,in order of preferences consists of prevention,minimization,recycling and reuse,biological treatment,incineration, and landfill disposal. The ideal idea of this project is to prevent waste generation in first place.Hence,waste prevention will be our basic goal in this project. Numerous technologies and manpower can be employed through this idea so that the wastes generated from the houses are picked up using the trucks and will be deposited to the respective site to prevent the waste Generation.

Often these efforts come about from identified trends or specific products and manpower. So the registered member can contact us and specify their waste amount and one of our men will take those wastes in an hour and deposit them to the respective dumping grounds.
